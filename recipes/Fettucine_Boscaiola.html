Fettucine Boscaiola

A pasta dish that is cooked in a white based cream sauce and contains bacon and mushrooms as the primary ingredients

Ingredient list

  • Fettucine (or pasta of choice)
  • Bacon
  • Thickened cream
  • Butter
  • Shallot
  • Button Mushrooms
  1. Fill pot with Boiling water and add serving size of pasta and cook for 6 minutes or until al dente.
  2. Slice Bacon and Button Mushrooms, Dice a Tablespoon or two of shallots depending on desired strength.
  3. Render bacon in a nonstick pan for 2 minutes, add diced Shallots for a further minute or until fragrant, add tablespoon of butter and half a cup of Mushrooms and saute Mushrooms until slight browning is achieved.
  4. Add thickened cream and reduce until cream is thick and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Strain and add pasta to pan before tossing/mixing.
  6. Plate and serve pasta.
