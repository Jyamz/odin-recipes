Teriyaki Sauce

A sweet and savoury soy based sauce/glaze that can be used to finish your protein of choice

Ingredient list

  • 250 ml Soy sauce
  • 200 ml Mirin
  • 200 ml Sake
  • 60-90g White sugar
  1. Weigh and add sugar to a saucepan.
  2. Combine all wet ingredients and set on stove.
  3. Heat mixture over a medium-low flame and stir until sugar is dissolved whilst being careful to not bring to a boil.
  4. Store sauce into a bottle or jar and refrigerate, Sauce can be resued and poured back into container to develop more flavour after recipe.
